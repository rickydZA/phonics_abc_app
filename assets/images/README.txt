RECOMMENDED SETTINGS:
- Format: WebP (best compression) or PNG (wider compatibility)
- Dimensions: 512x512 pixels (square)
- Background: Transparent (recommended) or solid color
- Color Mode: RGB
- Quality: 80-90% for WebP

IMAGE CONTENT TIPS:
- Show the uppercase letter prominently
- Include the associated object clearly visible
- Use bright, child-friendly colors
- Keep designs simple and recognizable
- Maintain consistent style across all 26 images

CREATION OPTIONS:

1. AI Image Generation:
   - DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion
   - Prompt example: "Cute cartoon letter A with a red apple,
     child-friendly, simple, bright colors, white background"

2. Stock Images:
   - Freepik, Flaticon, Shutterstock
   - Search: "alphabet letters for kids" or "phonics flashcards"

3. Create Your Own:
   - Canva (free, easy templates)
   - Adobe Illustrator / Photoshop
   - GIMP (free desktop app)
   - Procreate (iPad)

CONVERTING TO WEBP:
- Online: squoosh.app (free, no upload limits)
- Desktop: GIMP, Photoshop, or ImageMagick
- Command line: cwebp input.png -o output.webp -q 85
