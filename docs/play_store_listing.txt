APP NAME

English ABCs


SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 characters max)
--------------------------------------
Learn ABCs with phonics! Tap letters to hear sounds and see fun pictures.


FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
English ABCs is a fun, interactive phonics app designed for young learners to master the alphabet! Perfect for preschool and kindergarten children beginning their English language journey.

FEATURES:
• Complete A-Z alphabet with beautiful, child-friendly illustrations
• Tap any letter to hear clear phonics pronunciation
• Fun audio chants help children remember letter sounds (e.g., "A A a a a, a is for apple")
• Colorful picture associations for every letter (Apple, Ball, Cat, Dog, Frog, and more!)
• Simple, distraction-free interface perfect for young children
• Tap images to replay audio as many times as needed
• No ads, no in-app purchases - just pure learning fun
• Works offline - core learning features don't require internet

LEARNING BENEFITS:
✓ Letter recognition and phonics awareness
✓ Sound-letter association skills
✓ Vocabulary building with common words
✓ Independent learning - kids can explore at their own pace
✓ Multi-sensory learning through audio and visuals

PERFECT FOR:
• Preschool and kindergarten students (ages 3-8)
• ESL/EFL young learners of any age
• Parents teaching phonics at home
• Early childhood educators and teachers
• Speech therapists working on letter sounds
• Struggling readers needing phonics reinforcement

HOW IT WORKS:
Children simply tap any letter from A to Z to hear its phonics sound and see a related image in a popup. Tap the image to replay the audio as many times as needed! The repetitive audio chants reinforce learning through rhythm and repetition, making phonics fun and memorable.

Start your child's reading journey today with English ABCs!

完整說明
--------
English ABCs 是一款有趣的互動式自然發音應用程式，專為年幼學習者設計，幫助他們掌握英文字母！非常適合剛開始英語學習之旅的學齡前及幼稚園兒童。

功能特色：
• 完整的 A-Z 字母表，配有精美、適合兒童的插圖
• 點擊任何字母即可聽到清楚的自然發音
• 有趣的語音吟唱幫助孩子記住字母發音（例如：「A A a a a, a is for apple」）
• 每個字母都有彩色圖片聯想（蘋果 Apple、球 Ball、貓 Cat、狗 Dog、青蛙 Frog 等等！）
• 簡單、無干擾的介面，非常適合幼兒
• 點擊圖片可隨時重播音訊
• 無廣告、無應用程式內購買 - 純粹的學習樂趣
• 離線可用 - 核心學習功能不需要網路連線

學習效益：
✓ 字母辨識和自然發音意識
✓ 聲音與字母的關聯技能
✓ 透過常用單字建立詞彙量
✓ 自主學習 - 孩子可以按照自己的步調探索
✓ 透過聽覺和視覺的多感官學習

最適合：
• 學齡前和幼稚園學生（3-8 歲）
• 任何年齡的 ESL/EFL 年輕學習者
• 在家教授自然發音的家長
• 幼兒教育工作者和教師
• 協助兒童練習字母發音的語言治療師
• 需要加強閱讀能力的兒童

運作方式：
孩子只需點擊 A 到 Z 的任何字母，即可在彈出視窗中聽到其自然發音並看到相關圖片。點擊圖片可根據需要多次重播音訊！語音吟唱透過節奏和反覆練習加強學習，使自然發音變得有趣且令人難忘。

今天就開始您孩子的英語閱讀之旅吧！
