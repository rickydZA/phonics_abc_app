Introduction

English ABCs (also known as "Phonics ABC App") is a free educational application designed to help young children learn the English alphabet and basic phonics. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children, and comply with Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act and international children's privacy standards including COPPA.

English ABCs（亦稱為「字母拼讀應用程式」）是一款免費的教育應用程式，旨在幫助幼童學習英文字母和基礎拼讀。我們致力於保護使用者的隱私，特別是兒童，並遵守台灣個人資料保護法及國際兒童隱私標準（包括 COPPA）。
