Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Summary: Phonics ABC App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline and is safe for children.

Introduction

Phonics ABC App ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free educational application designed to help young children learn the English alphabet and basic phonics. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The app:

  • Does not require registration or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any personal data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not make automatic network requests

How the App Works

Phonics ABC App operates entirely on your device:

  • All audio and images are stored locally within the app
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • No data is sent to external servers

External Links

The app contains optional links to external educational resources (teacher website and podcast episodes). When you tap these links:

  • You will be taken to an external website in your device's browser
  • These external sites have their own privacy policies
  • We encourage parents to supervise children when accessing external content

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children and complies with children's privacy protection principles:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • No account creation is required
  • The app can be used without any personal data

Data Storage

The app does not store any user data, preferences, or usage history on your device or on any external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
